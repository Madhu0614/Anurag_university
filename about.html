1st International Conference on Sustainable Energy and Artificial Intelligence Driven Technologies for Next Generation Applications

ICSEAIDTNGA 2025

November 28-29, Anurag University, Hyderabad, India (Hybrid Mode)

About the Conference

The International Conference on Renewable Energy, Artificial Intelligence, and Sustainable Transportation serves as a dynamic platform that brings together leading researchers, scientists, engineers, and industry professionals from across the globe. The conference aims to foster knowledge exchange and promote collaboration on the latest innovations and trends shaping the future of clean energy, intelligent systems, and sustainable transport solutions.

In the face of global challenges such as climate change and resource depletion, renewable energy sources are essential to building a sustainable future. The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing how energy systems are managed, improving efficiency, predictive maintenance, and decision-making in energy grids and smart technologies. With AI-driven insights, advancements in renewable energy—from solar and wind to hydropower—can be optimized, addressing supply-demand challenges, and lowering emissions.

The conference will also focus on sustainable transportation solutions, exploring electric vehicles (EVs), autonomous driving technologies, and smart city infrastructures. It delves into how AI algorithms enhance EV performance, optimize charging infrastructure, and improve traffic management systems, contributing to greener cities.

By bringing together experts in these transformative fields, the conference aspires to foster interdisciplinary discussions, accelerate the development of innovative technologies, and build partnerships that will drive progress in renewable energy, AI, and sustainable transportation. It will be a pivotal event in shaping future research directions and policy-making efforts toward a cleaner, smarter, and more sustainable world.

About the University

Anurag University - Inspiring Tomorrows Founded in 1998 as Lalitha Degree and PG College, Anurag University has grown into a multidisciplinary institution dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation. The journey expanded with CVSR College of Technology (2002) and Lalitha College of Pharmacy (2005), culminating in the formation of Anurag Group of Institutions in 2010. In 2020, the institution transformed into Anurag University, one of Telangana's first state private universities, committed to shaping future leaders and changemakers. Spread across 150+ acres, Anurag University is home to 15,000+ students, 500+ Doctorate faculty, and 50+ programs at UG, PG, and Doctoral levels. The university operates through six schools.

With a focus on innovation, global exposure, and industry-driven learning, Anurag University provides world-class infrastructure, cutting-edge research facilities, and strong international collaborations. The school of Pharmacy is ranked 71st in India by NIRF, and other programs consistently rank in the 101-150 band nationally. Anurag University fosters a vibrant campus life, offering students opportunities to grow academically and personally through mentorship programs, student clubs, and engaging activities. With modern labs, industry partnerships, and a strong placement network, the university equips students with the skills to thrive in a competitive world. From exciting events to top-tier placements, Anurag University continues to be a hub of innovation, research, and holistic education, inspiring the leaders of tomorrow.

About the Department

The Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering is established in 2002. Over a period of 21 years the department has been a hive of academic activity and instrumental in creating successful careers for many students.

The department offers:

  • B.Tech in EEE (Intake: 120)
  • PG in Power Electronics and Electrical Drives (PEED)
  • Ph.D. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • Honors & Minors in Electric Vehicles and Smart Grids

Department clubs and professional chapters (IEEE, ISTE, IEI) for student’s professional development. Consistent growth was witnessed in development and consequently the Department was accredited by NBA in 2009, 2017 & 2022 and reaccredited by NBA in 2025 under Tier-1. Very committed and experienced faculty lead the student development activities supported by an active team of well qualified faculty. Good Infrastructure and laboratory facilities to the students to access with latest technologies and equipment. The academic environment of the Department encourages the students to think innovatively and develop engineering solutions to address societal problems.
