ISTE sponsored 2nd International Conference on Renewable Energy,

Green Computing & Sustainable Development

Engineering Perspectives for Sustainability

ICREGCSD 2025

February 21-22, Anurag University, Hyderabad, India (Hybrid Mode)

Call for Papers - ICREGCSD 2025

Themes

Track 1: Power Electronics For Energy Applications

  • Solar, wind, and hybrid energy systems
  • Energy storage technologies and management systems
  • Integration of renewable energy with conventional grids
  • Economic and environmental impacts of renewable energy systems
  • Power electronic converters and their applications
  • Wide-bandgap semiconductor devices and circuits
  • High-frequency and high-efficiency power conversion
  • Design and development of advanced inverter and rectifier systems

Track 2: Advances in Cyber Physical Systems And Smart Grids

  • Power system optimization
  • Power quality issues and mitigation techniques
  • Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTS) and custom power devices
  • Grid stability, smart grids, and microgrid innovations
  • Cyber physical and cyber security in power systems
  • AI and ML algorithms for energy forecasting and optimization
  • Evolutionary algorithms for electrical system design
  • Digital twins and smart monitoring systems in power applications
  • Signal processing applications in energy systems

Track 3: Sustainable Transportation And Energy Technologies

  • Battery Management System
  • Fast charging solutions
  • Renewable Energy Integration with Electric Vehicle Topology
  • Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) and Grid-to-Vehicle (G2V) Technologies
  • Electric Drive Systems
  • Transportation electrification and charging infrastructure
  • Electric vehicle powertrains and battery management systems
  • AI-driven automotive technologies and control systems
  • Automation and control systems for electric and autonomous vehicles

Track 4: AI Approaches for Sustainable Future in Energy

  • AI approaches for Smart grid optimization and stability in power system
  • Machine learning in Predictive maintenance for Renewable energy systems
  • Energy management in Distributed power networks using AI
  • AI for Real-Time Monitoring and Control of Energy Grids
